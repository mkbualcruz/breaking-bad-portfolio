About Me

From Chemistry Teacher to Kingpin: The Evolution of Walter White

My name is Walter White, formerly a high school chemistry teacher, now a man who has harnessed the full potential of science and ambition. What began as a desperate attempt to secure my family's future after a terminal cancer diagnosis evolved into something much more. I am meticulous, methodical, and driven by a need for control, legacy, and respect.

I once taught teenagers about covalent bonds and the periodic table. Now, I am the one who knocks. Operating under the alias Heisenberg, I built an empire with nothing but a battered RV, a former student, and a formula that redefined purity in the trade. Every decision I’ve made—better or worse—was calculated, precise, and necessary.
